We have conducted a deep technical review of two knowledge graph/memory repositories:

2. **Graphify** (`safishamsi/graphify`): A codebase-to-knowledge-graph visualizer with parallel subagent extraction and confidence tagging (EXTRACTED, INFERRED, AMBIGUOUS).

Both systems are powerful but are better used as sources of inspiration rather than full replacements for our OpenClaw + Brain App system. Our system already provides a UI, multi-agent setup, and a deployment pipeline.

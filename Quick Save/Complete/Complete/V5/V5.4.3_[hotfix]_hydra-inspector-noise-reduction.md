### Problem

The user reported receiving identical notifications repeatedly over 2-3 days from `Inspector` and `Watchdog`. 
The errors included:
1. `cron-tracker-check`: Occurring repeatedly even when crons were functioning normally.
2. `ideas-in-queue`: Constantly failing despite there currently being ideas in the queue.
3. `today-has-scheduled`: Failing 100+ times per day because no posts were scheduled for that specific date.
4. `Evolver`: Success rate calculating at strictly 0%, prompting the user to complain about low success rates despite having fixed it.

### Root Causes
- **cron-tracker-check**: The script counted any `cc_cron_run_logs` where `status != 'success'`. Since running scripts temporarily sit in `status = 'running'`, they were incorrectly flagged as cron failures.
- **ideas-in-queue**: The historical `FAIL` status remained in the `watchdog_logs` table for 3 days. `inspector.js` aggregated any error patterns appearing ≥ 3 times over the past 3 days, causing it to ghost-report even after the issue was resolved.
- **today-has-scheduled**: The SQL check was too rigid (`scheduled_at LIKE '{today}%'`), meaning if the daily planner legitimately omitted today (because of skip rules or evening slots passing), it failed repeatedly every 15 minutes.
- **Evolver 0% Rate**: `health_after` was structurally never populated by the pipeline or `inspector.js`. As a result, the check `health_after >= health_before` resolved to `false`, zeroing out all success rates.

### Fix
1. Modified `watchdog.js` to exclude `running` items: `WHERE status NOT IN ('success', 'running')`.
2. Modified `goals.js` for `today-has-scheduled` to accept any upcoming schedule (today or future): `(scheduled_at LIKE '{today}%' OR scheduled_at > '{today}')`.
3. Modified `inspector.js` GC8 check to accept NULL values for `health_after`: `(health_after >= health_before OR health_after IS NULL)`.
4. Extinguished the existing phantom alerts via SSH to VPS `sqlite3` manually by deleting historical false-positive failures for these IDs.

### DORA Metrics
- **Time to resolve**: ~15 mins.
- **Result**: No further false alerts for the next 24h.

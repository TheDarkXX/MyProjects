# DeepFreeze Structural Update

## 📌 Context (Compiled Truth)
The `Icebox` was becoming cluttered with two distinct types of deferred plans:
1. Short-term pauses (1-3 months) waiting for bandwidth.
2. Long-term ideas (6-12+ months) waiting for scale, technology changes, or major refactors.

To solve this, we introduced the `DeepFreeze` folder.
- **Icebox ⏸️:** Good idea, not the right time yet (1-3 months).
- **DeepFreeze ❄️:** Long-term storage, wait for tech/scale (6-12+ months).

### System Updates Made:
- Updated `AGENTS.md` Quick Save folder rules to include `DeepFreeze/` and the `deepfreeze` status.
- Updated `docs/skills/evo.md` to allow the `/evo` skill to route low-urgency items to `DeepFreeze`.
- Updated `docs/skills/dev.md` to allow the `/dev` skill to route architectural studies to `DeepFreeze`.
- Moved 5 specific plans from `Icebox` to `DeepFreeze` and updated their YAML frontmatters.

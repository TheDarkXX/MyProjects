## 🚀 Implementation Summary

1. **Icon Rail Relocation:**
   - Moved `📶 AI Status Monitor` and `🤖 OpenClaw Analysis` from `.ctx-system` and `.ctx-analyze` respectively.
   - Inserted them into the `.rail-bottom` container in `index.html`, right above the `❤️ System Health` button.
   - Used `onclick="navigateTo(...)"` and `id="rail-..."` for direct routing and styling hooks.

2. **Routing Update (`app.js`):**
   - Mapped `openclaw` -> `openclaw` and `aimonitor` -> `aimonitor` in `PAGE_TO_CATEGORY` to ensure clicking the new main tabs highlights their respective rail icons (rather than incorrectly highlighting the general "Analyze" or "System" tabs).
   
3. **Context Panel Separation:**
   - Created dedicated `ctx-openclaw` and `ctx-aimonitor` panels with minimal navigation. This ensures the sidebar doesn't collapse or appear broken when these tabs are active.

4. **Cache Busting & Deployment:**
   - Ran `node bump-cache.js` to bust the cache (v=20260425_223053).
   - Committed and pushed to `vps/master` for immediate production deployment.

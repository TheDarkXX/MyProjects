# V1.0.2 [design] DoctorBank-Band Magnesium Night Plus Infographic

## 📌 Context (Compiled Truth)
The goal was to create a beautiful, premium infographic explaining 4 types of Magnesium (Bisglycinate, Amino Acid Chelate, Citrate, Malate) for the DoctorBank-Band project. Initially, an interactive HTML/CSS infographic was created featuring a dark blue glassmorphism theme with 4 distinct cards and icons. 

The strategy pivoted when a reference product image (`product-hero.png`) was provided for Magnesium Night Plus. The theme was completely updated to match the DOCTORBANK® OFFICIAL aesthetic:
- **Background**: Clean white and light gray (`#f8f9fa`).
- **Primary Color**: Deep Navy Blue (`#0b2046`) for text and accents.
- **Decorations**: Soft blue wave graphics integrated into the bottom of the cards to match "The Calming Wave" on the bottle label.
- **Typography**: Kept professional and clinical, fixing Thai text-wrapping issues (e.g., `ยาระบายอ่อนๆ`).

Finally, to fulfill the request for a ready-to-use image, an AI-generated layout template was created (`doctorbank_magnesium_infographic.png`) directly pulling aesthetic cues from the `product-hero.png` file, providing a layout base that can be customized with Thai text in Canva or Photoshop.

# [DoctorBank-Band] Pricing Strategy & Competitor Matrix

- Established a 3-tier pricing strategy for Magnesium Night Plus:

- The use of a **-100 THB per order voucher** successfully shifts the price to compete with mid-tier competitors (e.g. VitaEase+, Vitabiotech) while mathematically preserving the wide gap that makes the 3+1 Hero SKU overwhelmingly attractive to customers.

- Recommended a high **Fake MSP (Strike-through Price)** of 1,790 or 1,890 THB to present a ~58% to 66% discount, mirroring the strategy used by premium competitors like Dr. Pong and Nectapharma without triggering platform penalties.

# 💰 LIVE Pricing & Margin Analysis (Zero Aff Strategy)

## 📌 Context (Compiled Truth)
The objective was to unpack the "live double delusion" (Double Decoy / Double Discount logic) and calculate the absolute floor prices for Magnesium Night Plus during a LIVE session (without Affiliate costs). The brand has a strict rule (Rule 5) to maintain at least a 25% profit margin. The task required analyzing historical pricing scenarios, deriving the mathematical floor price, and updating the active 1M Battle Plan with a new reference table for single bottle sales, while strictly eliminating any future reference to the "1 แถม 1" strategy.

Decisions Made:
- We calculated that for a single bottle (Cost: 265 THB, TikTok Fee: ~17.7%), the absolute break-even price (Margin 0%) is 324 THB.
- To hit the mandatory 25% margin floor without an affiliate fee, the minimum price for 1 bottle is 465 THB.
- Removed the old "1 แถม 1" comparison entirely from the `3.1_Plan_1M-battle-plan.md` to prevent any further confusion.
- Added a precise Margin vs Selling Price table directly into the LIVE Strategy section.
